Wait, what is this?

Welcome to Find Your Next Favourite, a film recommendation app based on Letterboxd and Just Watch Data. Simply input your username or a collection of usernames, and Find Your Next Favourite will present a curated selection of similar films from your watchlist. With convenient filtering and sorting options, including streaming services you're subscribed to, making a movie choice has never been easier. Find Your Next Favourite serves as a pivotal feature within the expansive "Films Wrapped", soon to come.
